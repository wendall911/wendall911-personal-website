The acquired startup vinamor.com was the first online-based wine sales company in the US. The owners approached me with a host of problems they were experiencing with their new startup. Having already received their first round of funding, they struggled with growth, as their existing IT infrastructure was inconsistent with their growth targets. This project required an end-to-end architecture of the entire IT infrastructure. This included a completely redesigned website with a custom shopping cart system heavily focused on SEO. The custom shopping cart system must be fully integrated with the warehouse systems and UPS. I worked with UPS for several months to create an efficient end-to-end solution for shipping wine, solving a major issue in that we needed special pickup and delivery windows, shipping containers and storage while in transit. On the marketing side, I implemented a complete solution for delivering newsletters and other email and direct mail marketing materials. I built a custom studio for producing audio and video podcast/vidcast productions that are fully integrated into the broader technology stack, including website and email. I successfully rearchitected the IT infrastructure, which resulted in the growth of over $1M in sales over 12 months.
