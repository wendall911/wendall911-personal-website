Torus Technologies planned and hosted many education events over the first few years since its establishment. Having moved into a much larger office space, I decided to convert the space into a technology center and co-working space. Sandpoint, Idaho, is the home of [Schweitzer Mountain](https://www.schweitzer.com/), a winter destination for skiing and snowboarding. We hosted many traveling engineers and community members (often non-profits) who lacked office space for their business needs. We had robust wifi, network connections and a guest computer system for the community members.
