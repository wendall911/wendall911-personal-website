I led two major technology projects for two real estate companies in Sandpoint, Idaho, Idaho First Real Estate and [North Idaho Rentals](https://www.northidahorentals.com/). Idaho First is no longer in business, as the owner has since retired. In addition to business consulting for IT needs, I built custom real estate property management software for both companies. This work required full integration of the local real estate MLS data with the ability to augment it with custom data, photos, and other internal financial data. The data was utilized to drive the front-end UI for both websites. I provided training and technical support for all staff at both businesses on using and maintaining the custom software for managing the site listings and other content.
