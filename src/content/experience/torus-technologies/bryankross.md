[bryankross.com](http://bryankross.com) is the sculpture website for figure artist Bryan K. Ross. I worked with Bryan on a business marketing strategy for the sales of his sculptures and prints, which resulted in the need for a website to showcase his work. I built the original website and was responsible for all the photography. I'm pleased that the site still showcases my personal design work and photography from the original site, even as it exists today. Features like the ability to change the background of the sculptures in a single click are examples of features I worked closely with Bryan on developing.
