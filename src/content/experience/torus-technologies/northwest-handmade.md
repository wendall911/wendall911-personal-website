[Northwest Handmade Furniture](https://www.northwesthandmade.com/) started as a local handmade furniture store based in Sandpoint, Idaho. The business had grown to the point that it wanted to expand its reach, which included a new branding and marketing plan. I was an expert consultant, directing staff in a new branding image. This included branding guidelines, a logo, a website, a newsletter and print marketing materials. The website was custom-designed to accommodate listings for external partners and inventory for two storefronts.
