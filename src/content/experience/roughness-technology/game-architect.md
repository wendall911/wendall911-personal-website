Over the last few years, I developed multiple games and game modifications, from concept to delivery, with millions of downloads. Developing educational games or mods for existing games with an educational focus is a complex process that requires research and planning, given the target audience and educational concept.

One success has been an educational modification or "mod" for the popular game Minecraft. This mod uses a sophisticated temperature simulation system to mimic homeostasis and weather systems. It teaches learners of all ages how homeostasis works in the human body and how it relates to the outdoor environment.

I develop accessibility-compliant game software that provides translations into multiple languages. Both accessibility and translation support require coordination with numerous outside experts to ensure the quality meets rigorous education standards.
