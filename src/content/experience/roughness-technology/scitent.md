I worked with [Scitent](https://www.scitent.com/), a prominent eLearning company, as the principal architect for an innovative solution to migrate an existing assessment delivery system to a scalable cloud architecture. The existing infrastructure needed to be updated, allowing scalability and flexibility to support their wide range of products and services. My primary role was to design and implement a complex backend API, front-end user interface requirements and infrastructure deployment models for migration. While working with Scitent, I was key in adding staff positions to work as direct hires within the organization. I was directly responsible for training and managing this staff for the initial switch to the new technology stack.
