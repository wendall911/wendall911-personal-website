While at [ETS](https://www.ets.org/), I was a primary author on the National Assessment for Educational Progress (NAEP) assessment delivery platform contract, which resulted in a $50M award to ETS over a 5-year contract cycle. Given my reputation and unique expertise, [Manhattan Strategy Group](https://manhattanstrategy.com/) approached me to act as a subject matter expert for a new federal contract to host the Department of Education [Nations Report Card](https://www.nationsreportcard.gov/) and affiliated [NCES](https://nces.ed.gov/) websites and internal software products.

My initial engagement was to educate company leadership on the unique challenges of this federal contract. In the proposal authoring phase, I authored sections that posed significant technical challenges to the organization. As a primary reviewer of all contract language, I played a crucial role in keeping contract language consistent with the proposal goals.
