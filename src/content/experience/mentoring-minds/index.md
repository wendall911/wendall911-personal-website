Mentoring Minds – before Curriculum Associates bought them – was in the process of purchasing a small EdTech startup, MySatori. MySatori had created several products over the years but was solely responsible for the online delivery of educational content developed by Mentoring Minds in book form in an online format. MySatori needed someone with expertise in business strategy, education and software architecture to work with Mentoring Minds on the acquisition. I hired and expanded the existing engineering team, adding several new staff engineers, QC experts and project management staff. Additionally, I was responsible for evaluating the existing code base and ensuring the quality would be fine during and after the acquisition process.

I continued as Senior Software Architect after Mentoring Minds successfully acquired MySatori. It's been my favorite job throughout my career. I felt validated in all aspects of my expertise. We had a functioning and productive team utilizing agile methodologies and processes. I managed the release engineering and ensured the quality and reliability of our SAAS products. This opportunity led me to believe I could significantly impact an organization as an individual contributor and the boss.
