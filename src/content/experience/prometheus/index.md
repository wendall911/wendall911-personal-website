Typically, I work primarily on education-focused projects, but [Prometheus](https://prometheusapartments.com/), a real estate company, approached me with a problem they wanted my help with. They had become vendor-locked with several companies in the real estate world that they, as a certified B-Corp, were uncomfortable with. Given the wide variety of education-focused projects and my background in the construction industry, I agreed to serve as the principal software architect in this endeavor.

Prometheus does not have a dedicated technology team for most of its technology infrastructure. The IT department is almost solely devoted to supporting its internal business needs, such as offices, apartments and businesses. One of my first roles was to work with external vendors and partners to address the current needs and form a new engineering team. Working with multiple outside vendors, I was the primary point of contact and manager for a 10+ person engineering team, acting as liaison for the external software engineering partners.

By the end of this project, we successfully built the flexibility to move between product providers to better align with business requirements, vision and philosophy.
