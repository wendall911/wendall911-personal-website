Apple, Inc. approached us with a unique challenge. Having partnered with [Osirix](https://www.osirix-viewer.com/) to help with the Mac-based versions of its medical viewer, they wanted a website dedicated to showcasing the imaging software's capabilities. In this partnership, I led the development of a custom website to showcase the viewer software's capabilities. Eventually, Osirix built its web presence and built up on the success of the product showcase hosted by Apple.
