When hiring, I primarily am seeking one trait. Does the candidate know how to learn? I've found that teams filled with learners, not minions, are effective and efficient.

So, if there was one thing I think should be taught in schools, it is how to learn. Schools in the US are primarily focused on rote memorization. This doesn't help develop future scientists, engineers, or any other type of professional worker. This may have been a good model at some point, but it is a failed/failing model for the modern world.
