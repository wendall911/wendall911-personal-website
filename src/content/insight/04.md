I find that companies today cannot adapt and be agile. Many companies cannot move past the sunk cost fallacy. Just because a project was successful and relevant in the past doesn't mean it translates to the present. The world is changing rapidly, and the ability to adapt and be agile is a requirement.

Growing up with a family in the construction industry and learning how to build myself, I often find it perplexing how companies are so unwilling and unable to adapt to changes. When you own a house, and something needs to be changed, you remodel! When you've discovered a new hobby that requires more space, you build out. Repaint, remodel, expand, decorate ... iterate on things that work, remove things that don't. Be agile.
